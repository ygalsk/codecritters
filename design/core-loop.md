# Core Loop

1. **Hub** — manage your roster, equip [[items#Move Discs|move discs]] and [[items#Hold Items|hold items]], plan your party
2. **Party select** — pick 3 (or 4 after [[progression#Meta Progression|unlock]]) critters for the run
3. **Dungeon** — descend floor-by-floor through procedurally generated rooms (see [[dungeon]])
4. **Encounters** — walk into enemies (no escape). Fight, catch, or conserve. (see [[battle-system]])
5. **Boss** — every 5 floors: a boss team. Defeat it for a shop and full party heal.
6. **Extract or push** — keep going for better loot and XP, or bank what you have
7. **Run over** — catches kept, [[progression#Scars|scars]] applied, [[progression#Cooldowns|cooldowns]] start. Back to the hub.

The loop is short enough for a single session (15 floors ~ 45-60 min). The meta is long enough for dozens.

See [[emotional-arc]] for how a full run feels.
