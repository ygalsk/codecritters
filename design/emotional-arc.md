# Emotional Arc of a Complete Run

## Act 1: Floors 1-5 (Confidence)

Your starter demolishes the first commons with [[types|type advantage]]. You catch one [[critters|critter]] for the bench. HP starts mattering on floor 3-4. Floor 5 boss has a [[battle-system#Trainer Boss Teams|trainer team]] (2 critters). You barely make it with Goto grinding down the second critter with [[types#Status Effects|Deprecated]]. Shop. Exhale.

## Act 2: Floors 6-10 (Investment)

Full heal. Spend currency on a Breakpoint. Party is leveling toward [[critters#Evolution|evolution]] at level 12. Floor 7: a Heisenbug in a [[dungeon#Chest Room Rewards|chest room]] — you catch it on the third attempt. Floor 9: Goto takes a critical hit. First [[progression#Scars|scar]] screen. `-1 Logic — permanently.` You look at Goto differently now.

## Act 3: Floors 11-15 (Stakes)

Real danger. Every [[battle-system|encounter]] costs something. Floor 12 has a [[battle-system#Swarm|swarm room]] — you knew to expect it past floor 11. Floor 14: you see a chest room glow (rare loot indicator). You push for it. A CHAOS Disc III. Worth it. Floor 15: the final boss. Full trainer team. You win with your last Hotfix item.

## Run Summary

Run over: 6 catches, 2 scars, 1 evolution, deepest floor 15. Println has 1 scar. Goto has 1 scar. Next run: you know what the [[types|type chart]] does.
